🧠 Trauma & Recovery Tab - Deeper Insights

For people healing from *abuse, rejection, loss, heartbreak, or childhood pain.*

1. Do you often think about a painful past event, and does it feel like it happened recently?

2. Does the trauma affect your sleep or cause you to feel constantly "on guard" (hyper-vigilance)?

3. Do you find yourself avoiding people, places, or activities that remind you of the past event?

4. Do you believe you are intrinsically flawed or responsible for what happened (guilt/shame)?

5. Have you seriously considered talking to a trauma-informed professional?

6. Do you find it difficult to maintain close, trusting relationships since the event?

✅ Identified Need: Emotional trauma recovery.

💡 Recommended Healing Plan:

  • Start with gentle forgiveness exercises.
  • Write a letter to your younger self — release what you’ve carried.
  • Practice breathing & mindfulness for 10 mins daily.
  • Consider faith-based or professional counseling.
  • 📚 *Free Resource:* “Healing from Emotional Trauma – PDF Guide.”

🧠 Mental Strength Builder - Deeper Insights

Focus: Build positive habits, gratitude, and mindset for long-term wellness.

1. How often do you practice gratitude or intentionally celebrate a small win?

2. How do you primarily respond when you encounter a significant setback or failure?

3. Do you have clearly defined daily or weekly goals (for wellness, work, or spirit)?

4. How often do you engage in a spiritual or mindfulness routine (prayer, meditation, nature)?

5. How would you rate your ability to say "No" to non-essential commitments?

6. Do you prioritize time for creative outlets, hobbies, or things that purely bring you joy?

✅ Recommendation: Build Resilience Routine

💡 Daily Habits:

  • Gratitude journaling (3 things daily)
  • 10-min reflection/prayer
  • Limit negative media
  • 🎧 *Music Therapy:* Calm Piano or Lo-fi Gospel playlist
