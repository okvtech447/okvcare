🧠 Emotional Health Finder

For anyone feeling lost, down, or emotionally heavy. Complete the quiz below.

1. How have you been feeling lately?

2. How long have you felt this way?

✅ Emotional Insight: You may be dealing with emotional burnout or mild depression.

💬 Encouragement: You are not alone. What you feel is valid. Healing takes time.

💡 Suggested Actions:

  • Take short daily walks and stay connected with a trusted person.
  • Try journaling your feelings (2–3 sentences each morning).
  • If it persists, talk to a counselor or trained mentor.
